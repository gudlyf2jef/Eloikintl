About Eloik

Eloik International Communication Equipment Technology CO., Ltd from Germany specialize in research, development, production and sales of optical communication equipment. Our Assembling factory is located in Tianjin Economic Rim demostration base of private economic growth. Its main products are integrated with a number of patented multifunctional fiber slicing machines portable optical fiber splicer, and handheld optical fiber splicer. We have a strong R & D, team composed of experts with many years of photoelectric integration development experience. We also have rigorous high standard production control technology system, ISO9001 quality assurance system, and perfect after-sale service system.
